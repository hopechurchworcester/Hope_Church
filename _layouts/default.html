EXPORTING HOPE

We love to share what we have with others – regionally and internationally.

  • The Catalyst Network

    HOPE is part of The Catalyst Network - “a grouping of apostolic teams empowering churches to see God’s kingdom fill the earth” We serve our friends by serving a number of other churches and leaders in the U.K.

  • Travels

    In recent years we have taught and served churches and leaders in Aisia, D.R. Congo, Uganda, Rwanda, Bosnia, Bulgaria and Germany. We are particularly involved with partnering in East Africa with our friends in Life Ministries Christian Church.

  • Uganda / Rwanda

    We continue to help in training church leaders in Uganda with our partners. See also richinhope.com and follow link to “Exporting Hope”.

  • Business Support

    HOPE is thrilled to be involved in helping provide the dignity of employment, an income and sustainable businesses for many in poverty in Uganda. We do this by serving in business coaching and providing micro-loans. We call this “Kairos business support” See richinhope.com and follow link to “Kairos Business Support”.

The Granary

Worcester's Granary.

We are bringing Worcester’s historic granary store back into community use. Restoration work began in 2020 after a long process of purchasing and developing plans. This beautiful four storey city centre building was originally part of the Hill and Evans vinegar works, constructed about 1830. As well being functioning as a church hub on Sundays, The Granary will be used to Build Community, Help the Vulnerable and Enrich Culture in our wonderful city.

The exterior has been totally restored …. And the first two floors are now fully used by church and community. Our next phases involve raising funds to install a kitchen and to transform the top floor into a large meeting room. For booking enquiries please use our contact form.

For photographs of the building works, and up to date information, feel free to look at The Granary, Worcester Facebook page.
