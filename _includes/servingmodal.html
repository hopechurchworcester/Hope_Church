Encounter Camp

Encounter is for anyone with life controlling issues that need a breakthough from God. Men and women of all ages.

Each year we gather men and women from all around the U.K. for a week of safe, friendly camping. God cares for broken people and Encounter camp helps many take important steps toward wholeness.

Hope at Home

In chapter 10 of Hebrews (a book of the Bible), the author reminds us how important it is for us in our walk with God to frequently spend time together with other believers. This is backed up by the example Jesus gave us throughout his time on earth. Hope at Home is a network of smaller groups meeting regularly with the aim to:

  • Connect together to develop strong, meaningful relationships and authentic community
  • Build and encourage one another in our faith and gifting
  • Empower us to serve others and make disciples
To find out when the groups are meeting and where, login to MyChurchSuite > My Groups. To find more about Hope at Home speak to Chris and Ceressa Bateman or Joel and Hanne Thomas

Children's Work

Students & YAs

We welcome Students & YAs at HOPE.

We welcome students & YAs at HOPE and work hard to make you feel welcome, engaged and invested in. We plan to help you settle into church and into Worcester. You are welcome at any of our Sunday congregations, though most choose the 11.15am. We run a weekly group for students & YAs based at The Granary. We look forward to doing more and more together - so please come along, or get in touch to introduce yourself, and ask any questions.
