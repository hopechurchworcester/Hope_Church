The Granary

Worcester's Granary.

We have brought Worcester’s historic granary into community use. Restoration work began in 2020. This beautiful four storey city centre building was originally a grain store for the Hill and Evans vinegar works, constructed about 1830. As well as functioning as a church hub on Sundays, The Granary will be used to Serve the Vulnerable, Build Community and Enrich Culture in our wonderful city. Rooms are available to hire at hourly rates, for booking enquires please use our contact form or Messenger

Resources and Accessibility

We have a number of resources you may book along with room hire including Gopack tables, chairs, flip charts and smart TVs with HDMI connection. There is a lift to all floors with unisex toilets and an accessible toilet on each floor.
